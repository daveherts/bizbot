Refund Policy for BrewBeans Co.

We want you to love your coffee! If you are not completely satisfied with your order, please review our refund policy below.

- Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase.
- Coffee beans must be unopened to qualify for a return.
- If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please contact our customer service team within 7 days.
- Refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5 business days after receiving the returned item.
- For issues with freshness or quality, please contact support with your order number and description.

Contact: support@brewbeans.co
